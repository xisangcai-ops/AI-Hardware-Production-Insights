## üöÄ The Economic Challenge of AI Hardware
AI toys and companion robots represent a massive market opportunity, but they face a harsh reality: **cloud computing costs**. Running large, unoptimized models via the cloud destroys product margins and creates latency that ruins user experience.

To make AI hardware viable for the mass market, the intelligence must be moved to the "edge"‚Äîdirectly onto the hardware.

## üõ†Ô∏è What is Model Distillation?
Model distillation allows us to take a "teacher" model (a large, complex LLM) and train a "student" model (a lightweight, efficient version) that retains the core intelligence but requires a fraction of the computational power.

At **EdgeSOM**, we specialize in **[LLM Training & Model Distillation Services](https://www.edgesom.com/products_8/llm-training-model-distillation-services.html)**. This process enables:
* **Lower BOM Costs**: Running AI on $5-$10 domestic chipsets instead of expensive processors.
* **Privacy & Speed**: Millisecond response times with offline edge computing.
* **Emotional Engagement**: Balancing technical efficiency with warm, relatable interaction logic.
