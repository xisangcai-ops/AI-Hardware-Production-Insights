## Company Overview

EdgeSOM is a full-stack AI solution provider specializing in **AI toys, AI education robots, AI companion robots, and AI-powered smart devices**. We deliver **end-to-end services from AI research and development to mass production**, enabling startups and brands to bring AI hardware products to market faster, at significantly lower cost.

Our core strength lies in bridging **LLM training and model distillation** with **embedded AI, hardware design, PCBA, and ISO-certified mass manufacturing**.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. LLM Training & Model Distillation

* Custom large language model (LLM) training and fine-tuning
* Model distillation optimized for edge devices and low-power hardware
* Cost-efficient AI inference for consumer-grade products
* Scenario-based AI behavior design (education, companionship, emotional interaction)

### 2. Embedded AI & System Integration

* AI model deployment on SoC, MCU, and edge AI chips
* Firmware, OS, and AI algorithm co-design
* Stable, scalable, and mass-production-ready architectures

### 3. Hardware R&D to Mass Production

* Industrial design and electronic hardware design
* PCBA design, prototyping, and optimization
* ISO-certified factory mass production
* OEM / ODM support and supply-chain integration
* Factory visit and long-term manufacturing partnership
